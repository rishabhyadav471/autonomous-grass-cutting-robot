# Pin Connections

## Arduino UNO to Ultrasonic Sensor (HC-SR04)
- VCC  → 5V  
- GND  → GND  
- TRIG → Digital Pin 9  
- ECHO → Digital Pin 10  

## Arduino UNO to Motor Driver (L298N)
### Left Motor
- IN1 → Digital Pin 2  
- IN2 → Digital Pin 3  

### Right Motor
- IN3 → Digital Pin 4  
- IN4 → Digital Pin 5  

### Enable Pins
- ENA → Digital Pin 6 (PWM)

## Cutter Motor Control
- Relay IN → Digital Pin 8  
- Relay VCC → 5V  
- Relay GND → GND  

## Power Connections
- Motor Driver +12V → Battery Positive  
- Motor Driver GND → Battery Ground  
- Arduino GND → Common Ground with Motor Driver
